Dear Nete Staff:

This application contains an overview of my observations and the requested Excel test.

 

Background

The data was submitted on January 2, 2015 in an Excel file. It contained information on what appears to be financial grants issued to research institutions. By observation it included details for institution and amounts, along with other various supporting fields.

Within the spreadsheet there were two task. The first was to create a pivot table and chart. The second was to fill in a table with requested fields.

 

Highlights

  1. Transaction types may be seasonal. This appears to be the case for initial awards, supplements and carryovers.
  2. The HIV Research Branch leads in aggregate among all branches for the years 2013 and 2014.
  3. In general the institutions appear to be receiving higher amounts from 2013 to 2014. However, there were two notable exceptions that had unusual declines: Fred Hutchingson Cancer Research Center, and the University of Miami School of Medicine.
  4. May and August months have high supplement amounts.
  5. December has a large influx of carryover.

 

Recommendations

  1. Consider advising the institutions on transaction timing.
  2. Request additional analysis on the declines in item (3), above.
  3. Consider requesting further analysis on transaction types: Supplement and Carryover.
  4. Request further investigation on highlighted items (4) and (5)
  5. Consider requesting a graph study of the dataset as demonstrated in this visualization. This may give you deeper insights into interrelationships that may not be obvious through the status quo.

 

Backup

  1. Items (1) through (3) are backed up by the Tableau Data Visualization
  2. Data used for this report can be found in the Excel SpreadSheet. Please note that the former contains the requested test exercises.
  3. Excel 2011 was used. No slicing is avaible in this version of Excel.
