Dear Nete Staff:

This web application contains a summary of the submitted data in both tabular and visual form.

The goal of this application is to bring the following items to your attention:

  1. Transaction types may be seasonal. This appears to be the case for initial awards, supplements and carryovers.
  2. The HIV Research Branch leads in aggregate among all branches for the years 2013 and 2014.
  3. In general the institutions appear to be recieving higher amounts from 2013 to 2014. However, there are two notable exceptions: Fred Hutchingson Cancer Research Center and the University of Miami School of Medicine both had significant decreases.

 

Backup

  1. Items (1) and (3) are backed up by the Tableau Data Visualization
  2. Data used for this report can be found in the Excel SpreadSheet

 

Recommendations

  1. Advise institutions on transaction timing.
  2. Research the exceptions in item (3)
